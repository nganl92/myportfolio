MY STORY

**Hello! My name is Ngân Lang Hữu Tố, and I am 25 years old. I am currently studying abroad at Omotenashi Senmon in Japan, specializing in IT frontend development and graphic design. My passion lies in creating visually appealing and functional websites, combining technical skills with artistic creativity. In addition to web development and design, I am also learning UI/UX to enhance user experience in my projects. I believe that a well-designed interface not only looks beautiful but also improves usability and accessibility. Since I am studying in Japan, I am actively improving my Japanese skills, particularly in grammar and business communication. Understanding the language and culture helps me adapt better and opens up more opportunities for collaboration in the industry. I enjoy working on innovative projects that push my skills to the next level. I am always eager to learn new technologies, experiment with design trends, and connect with like-minded individuals. If you share the same interests, feel free to reach out—I’d love to exchange ideas and collaborate!**
